Light

  • has a light tan color
  • little to no oil on the roasted beans
  • highest acidity
  • brightest of the three roast levels
  • orginal characteristics most pronounced
  • flavor comes from the original beans
  • often used for cuppings

Medium

  • richer brown color than a light roast
  • some oil on the roasted beans
  • coffee’s qualities begin tso give way to the roast’s flavors and aromas
  • balance between acidity and body

Dark

  • darkest brown
  • oil on the roasted beans
  • notes of flavor come exclusively from the roast
  • popular in Europe, giving rise to roasts such as Italian and French
  • espresso roasts are also usually dark roasts
